School Biography

Al-Awwal Schools was established on the 16th of September, 2000, at Ileoba Bus Stop. At that time, the school operated from a temporary site and started with just 19 students. Over the years, the school grew steadily as more parents recognized the quality of education we provided. After three years, we moved to our permanent site located at No. 3 River Road, where we continued to expand. Eleven years ago, we proudly added a secondary school to our institution, which is now known as Al-Awwal College, Ogijo. Today, we remain committed to nurturing young minds and building a legacy of excellence in education.
