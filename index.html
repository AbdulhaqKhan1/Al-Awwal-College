Brief history of the School Proprietor

Mr. Adebiyi Jimoh Okanlawon

Mr. Adebiyi Jimoh Okanlawon, a native of Ibadan Southwest, Oyo State, was born in Ogijo, Ogun State, where he spent his early years. He began his educational journey in Ikorodu, Lagos State, where he attended both primary and secondary school. Driven by a passion for learning and service, he pursued further studies in Oyo, earning a Nigeria Certificate in Education (NCE) in Primary Education after three years of dedicated study. His commitment to education helped shape him into the disciplined and knowledgeable individual he is today. Following his graduation, he embarked on a new chapter in life, marrying in 2003. Over the years, he and his wife have been blessed with five wonderful children. Today, as the proprietor of Al-Awwal College, Ogijo, Mr. Adebiyi Jimoh Okanlawon continues to make a significant impact in the field of education. Through his leadership, he instills discipline, knowledge, and strong moral values in young learners. His dedication and vision remain the driving force behind the school’s growth and success.
